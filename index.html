Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 25/12/2025

At CitiFix, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used within the app. By using CitiFix, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to enable civic issue reporting and tracking:
  • Name & Email: Used for account creation, authentication, and complaint association.
  • Phone Number (Optional): Collected only if you choose to provide it.
  • City / Locality: Helps route complaints to the appropriate civic authority.

2. Complaint Information

When you report a civic issue, the following information may be collected:
  • Issue category and description
  • Photos uploaded or captured as evidence
  • Location of the reported issue (issue location, not your home address)
  • Date and time of submission

3. Voice Input (Optional)

CitiFix offers an optional speech-to-text feature to make complaint reporting faster.

Important: Voice input is used only to convert speech into text. Audio is not recorded, stored, or shared.

4. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used solely to:
  • Enable citizens to report civic issues
  • Attach accurate location details to reported issues
  • Route complaints to relevant civic authorities
  • Display complaint status and authority responses
  • Allow escalation or validation of unresolved issues
  • Generate anonymized analytics and insights
CitiFix does not use personal data for advertising or marketing.

5. Use of Device Features

CitiFix may access certain device features strictly for app functionality:
  • Internet: To communicate with servers, Firebase services, and fetch updates.
  • Location: To tag reported civic issues with accurate issue locations.
  • Photos & Storage: To allow users to upload or capture images as complaint evidence.
  • Microphone: Only if the user chooses to use voice-to-text while reporting an issue.
All access is user-initiated, purpose-limited, and never continuous.

6. Email-Based Complaint Routing

CitiFix uses an email-based system to forward civic complaints to the appropriate authorities.

When a complaint is submitted:
  • The app requests permission only to send emails from the user’s email account.
  • A secure access token is transmitted to the CitiFix server.
  • The server sends the complaint email on the user’s behalf.
  • CitiFix’s official email account is included in CC for tracking purposes.
CitiFix does not read or access the user’s email inbox.

Authority responses are retrieved only from CitiFix’s own email account and displayed inside the app to maintain transparency and complaint tracking.

7. Data Storage & Security

We use Firebase Authentication for secure login and identity management.

Firebase Firestore stores:
  • User profile information
  • Complaint details and status updates
Uploaded images are securely stored in Firebase Cloud Storage.

8. Data Sharing

CitiFix does not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Complaint details are shared only with relevant civic authorities for the purpose of issue resolution.

9. Public Visibility of Complaints

Reported civic issues may appear in a public feed within the app to improve visibility and enable community engagement such as upvotes.

Personal contact details and user identities are never displayed publicly.

10. Data Retention After Account Deletion

When a user requests account deletion:
  • All personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, and user ID) is permanently deleted.
  • The user account is removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Civic issues reported by the user may be retained in an anonymized form for transparency, analytics, and public accountability.
  • All personal identifiers are removed, and retained issue data is no longer linked to a user.
  • Location data retained represents only the location of the reported issue, not the user.

11. Children’s Privacy

CitiFix is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly.

12. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Permissions may also be revoked through device settings.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
