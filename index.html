Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Add Date]

At CitiFix, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used within the app. By using CitiFix, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to enable civic issue reporting:
  • Name & Email: Used for account creation, authentication, and complaint association.
  • Phone Number (Optional): Collected only if you choose to provide it.
  • City / Locality: Helps route complaints to the appropriate civic authority.

2. Complaint Information

When you report a civic issue, we may collect:
  • Issue category and description
  • Photos uploaded or captured as evidence
  • Location of the reported issue
  • Date and time of submission

3. Voice Input (Optional)

CitiFix offers an optional speech-to-text feature to make complaint reporting faster.

Important: Voice input is used only to convert speech into text. Audio is not recorded, stored, or shared.

4. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used to:
  • Enable users to report civic issues
  • Attach accurate location details to complaints
  • Route complaints to relevant civic authorities
  • Display complaint status and authority responses
  • Allow escalation or validation of unresolved issues
  • Generate anonymized analytics and insights
CitiFix does not use your data for advertising or marketing.

5. Email-Based Complaint Routing

CitiFix uses an email-based system to forward complaints to the appropriate civic authorities.

When a complaint is submitted:
  • The app requests permission only to send emails from your email account
  • A secure access token is sent to the CitiFix server
  • The server sends the complaint email on your behalf
  • CitiFix’s official email account is included in CC for tracking
CitiFix does not read or access your email inbox.

Authority replies are retrieved only from CitiFix’s own email account and shown in the app for transparency and complaint tracking.

6. Data Storage & Security

We use Firebase Authentication for secure login and identity management.

Firebase Firestore stores:
  • User profile details
  • Complaint information and status
Uploaded images are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage.

7. Data Sharing

CitiFix does not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Complaint details are shared only with relevant civic authorities for the purpose of issue resolution.

8. Public Visibility of Complaints

Reported issues may appear in a public feed within the app to improve visibility and enable community engagement. Personal contact details are never shown publicly.

9. Children’s Privacy

CitiFix is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is identified, it will be deleted promptly.

10. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your account and associated data at any time. Permissions can also be revoked through device settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
